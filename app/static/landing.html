Features

Async Concurrent Scraping

Parallel scraping with configurable concurrency, rate limiting per outlet, and circuit breakers to handle failures gracefully.

Full Content Extraction

Goes beyond headlines. Extracts full article body, images, videos, author info, structured data, review scores, and more.

Adaptive scheduling adjusts scrape frequency per outlet based on publishing patterns. Retry queue handles transient failures.
